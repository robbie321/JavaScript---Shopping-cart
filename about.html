How it works

Donation

  1. List a pre-loved piece of furniture
  2. Have it collected from your home for free and delivered to a talented upcycler
  3. Receive 10% of the sale price. Sale price depends on size. €25 for small, €50 for medium and €75 for large

Upcycled piece

  1. Purchase a donation from the website that you would like to upcycle
  2. Give the piece a new lease of life, in line with ReFurno's style guide
  3. Relist it on the website to be sold at a price of your choice
  4. Once sold, have it collected and delivered to the buyer for free
  5. Keep 90% of the profits

FAQ's

What is ReFurno?
We’re a group of young women who are passionate about offering a sustainable solution to the harmful environmental effects of mass produced ‘fast furniture’. We strongly believe that the solution is simple; extending the life of existing pieces of furniture that were beautifully crafted years ago.

How does ReFurno work?
ReFunk allows you to donate, upcycle, and buy or sell upcycled furniture. We’re the one-stop shop for the circular furniture economy.

How do you price your pieces?
Our upcyclers decide the final price of the pieces they complete, we believe this is the fairest way to value their work and skill

What kind of furniture do you accept?
At present, to ensure top-quality, we are only accepting wooden furniture. However, we do plan to expand into upholstery in the future.

Will you upcycle my own furniture and give it back to me?
Yes! ReFunk accepts pieces your own and can put them through our upcycling matching process. Everything works the same, except you are providing the furniture and that will be reflected in the price.

Do you do commissions?
Yes, If you want a specific design or piece, whether it’s on a donation or your own piece, please contact us through instagram DMs or at our email refunkapp@gmail.com . You’ll let us know what you have in mind, and then we’ll let our ucpcyclers know what you’re looking for and for how much, and together we’ll find someone appropriate for the job. Or, you can request an upcycler by name if you’ve seen their work before (for example on our instagram or website).

We’ll then put you directly in touch with the upcycler, and you can go through design specifics and agree a final price. Once the piece is done we’ll take care of getting it to you. It’s as easy as that!

You can also ask us to keep an eye out for a type of donation in the future, if we don’t have it in stock now, such as a specific chair design.
